Different Types Of Coral In Fish Tanks

Overview

    2-8-2024

In coral reefs there is three main types of coral. these three types of coral are made up by

  1. SPS (Small polyp stoney)
  2. LPS (Large polyp stoney)
  3. Soft coral

These three main catagories of coral are what make up the reef tanks kept in people homes, and large aquarium facilities. Some people see coral as a plant, but coral is really a large colony of small organisms. Coral also isnt photosynthetic like some people thing, it actually has a symbyotic relationship with a photosyntheic bacteria that gives it energy in exange for a place to live and protection.

SPS

Small polyp stony corals are what make up most of what you find in the ocean, their anatomy consists of a hard calcium skeleton and many small feeding and photosynthetic polyps. SPS coral tend to be the hardest to keep due to their need for very stable water perameters and high light/flow. In this catagory there are many different types of SPS coral including

  1. Anacropora
  2. Montipora
  3. Plate Coral
  4. Birds Nest
  5. Acropora

Within these types of coral there is an unbelivable abundance of any color you can think of.

LPS

Just like the SPS coral the LPS coral has a calcium skeleton but they are different in the fact that they have Large polyps that extend out of their skeleton to feed and conduct photosynthesis, LPS are eisier to keep than SPS but still need a stable tank with good flow and lighting, some of these corals include

  1. Hammer Coral
  2. Duncan Coral
  3. Candycane Coral
  4. Gonipora
  5. Torch coral

The LPS coral still comes in a wide range of different colors, while also being able to move around with the flow of the system.

Image by 123RF

Soft Coral

instead of having a calcium skeleton, soft coral is made up of tissue, that feels like leather. leather comes in every shape size and color, from tall and stalky or wide and short. soft coral is the easiest and fastest growing, needing less light and flow than other LPS and SPS coral. some soft coral types are

  1. Zoanthids
  2. Leather corals
  3. Gorgonian
  4. Patlythoas
  5. pulsing zenia

certain soft corals like zoanthids and palythoas have a toxin called palytoxin, this toxin is able to kill people if ingested. the corals release this when stressed out.
