Different Types Of Coral In Fish Tanks

LPS

Just like the SPS coral the LPS coral has a calcium skeleton but they are different in the fact that they have Large polyps that extend out of their skeleton to feed and conduct photosynthesis, LPS are eisier to keep than SPS but still need a stable tank with good flow and lighting, some of these corals include

  • Hammer Coral
  • Duncan Coral
  • Candycane Coral
  • Gonipora
  • Torch coral

    • The LPS coral still comes in a wide range of different colors, while also being able to move around with the flow of the system.
